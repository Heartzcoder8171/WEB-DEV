Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum complex in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India, built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century as a tribute to his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. It is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world and a symbol of Indian history. Location and Coordinates The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India, at the coordinates 27°10′30″N 78°02′31″E. Area and Height The complex covers an area of 17 hectares (42 acres) and the main mausoleum stands at a height of 73 meters (240 feet).
